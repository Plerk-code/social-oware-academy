## ðŸŽ£ Paywall Strategy

### Paywall Timing (Contextual Triggers)

**1. Value Moment: After Completing Free Lessons**
- **Trigger:** Player completes Lesson 5 (last free lesson)
- **Context:** They've enjoyed learning, want more
- **Paywall Message:**
  - **Headline:** "Ready for Advanced Strategy?"
  - **Body:** "Unlock 10 more lessons with Premium. Master Oware like a pro!"
  - **CTA:** "Start 7-Day Free Trial"
  - **Dismissible:** Yes

**2. Pain Point: After 3rd Ad View**
- **Trigger:** Free player sees 3rd interstitial ad
- **Context:** Ads are annoying, they're engaged enough to see 3
- **Paywall Message:**
  - **Headline:** "Tired of Ads?"
  - **Body:** "Go ad-free with Premium. Plus get 2x XP and advanced lessons!"
  - **CTA:** "Try Premium Free for 7 Days"
  - **Dismissible:** Yes (X button)

**3. Loss Aversion: When Streak Breaks**
- **Trigger:** Player opens app, sees streak reset to 0
- **Context:** They feel loss, want to prevent future breaks
- **Paywall Message:**
  - **Headline:** "Never Lose Your Streak Again"
  - **Body:** "Premium includes 2 automatic streak freezes per month!"
  - **CTA:** "Protect My Streak"
  - **Dismissible:** Yes

**4. Achievement Moment: After Reaching Level 10**
- **Trigger:** Player levels up to Level 10
- **Context:** They're invested, feel proud, want to progress faster
- **Paywall Message:**
  - **Headline:** "You're Leveling Up Fast! ðŸŽ‰"
  - **Body:** "Premium gives you 2x XP. Reach Level 20 in half the time!"
  - **CTA:** "Accelerate My Progress"
  - **Dismissible:** Yes

**5. Competitive Pain: After Losing 3 Ranked Matches**
- **Trigger:** Player loses 3 ranked matches in a row
- **Context:** They want to improve, need better tools
- **Paywall Message:**
  - **Headline:** "Learn from the Pros"
  - **Body:** "Premium unlocks advanced strategy lessons and expert AI practice!"
  - **CTA:** "Improve My Game"
  - **Dismissible:** Yes

**6. Social Incentive: When Creating 4th Private Lobby**
- **Trigger:** Free player tries to create 4th lobby (limit is 3)
- **Context:** They're social, want to play with more friends
- **Paywall Message:**
  - **Headline:** "Unlock Unlimited Lobbies"
  - **Body:** "Premium lets you create as many private lobbies as you want!"
  - **CTA:** "Upgrade to Premium"
  - **Dismissible:** No (must dismiss or upgrade, or delete old lobby)

## ðŸŒ Regional Pricing (Future Consideration)

**Problem:** $6.99/month is expensive in emerging markets (Nigeria, India, Brazil)

**Solution: Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) Pricing**
- Adjust price by region based on local purchasing power
- Example:
  - USA: $6.99/month
  - Nigeria: $2.99/month (NGN 1,200)
  - India: $2.49/month (INR 199)
  - Brazil: $3.99/month (BRL 20)

**Implementation:**
- Unity IAP supports regional pricing
- PlayFab tracks player country
- Set custom prices per App Store region

**Risk Mitigation:**
- VPN abuse (players in USA buying at Nigeria prices)
- Mitigation: Payment method verification (must match country)
