## Persona 1: Maya "The Climber" (ACHIEVER - 40%)

### Demographics
- **Age:** 24
- **Location:** Brooklyn, NY
- **Occupation:** Marketing coordinator
- **Gaming Background:** Plays mobile puzzle games (Wordle, NYT Games), casual Chess.com user

### Psychographics
**Bartle Type:** ACHIEVER
**Motivations:**
- Visible progress and status
- Unlocking content through mastery
- Climbing ranks and beating personal bests
- Collecting achievements and badges

**Frustrations:**
- Games that don't reward time investment
- Unclear progression systems
- Pay-to-win mechanics
- Boring grinding without meaningful rewards

### Player Journey with Social Oware Academy

**Discovery:** Friend shares "Just hit Gold rank!" post on Instagram with game link

**Day 1:**
- Downloads app, selects "New to Oware"
- Completes guided first match (wins, feels smart)
- Sees clear progression path (Bronze rank, Level 1)
- Completes 2 more matches, reaches Level 2, unlocks first achievement
- **Hook:** "Only 3 more wins to Silver rank!"

**Week 1:**
- Plays 3-5 matches daily during commute
- Completes daily puzzles for bonus XP
- Maintains 7-day streak
- Reaches Silver rank, shares achievement
- **Hook:** Sees Premium offers 2x XP boost

**Month 1:**
- Converts to Premium (wants to level up faster)
- Unlocks advanced lessons
- Climbs to Gold rank
- Joins leaderboard top 500
- **Retained:** Multiple progression tracks keep her engaged

## Design Implications

### For Achievers (40% - Maya):
**Priority Features:**
- Multi-track progression (Level, Rank, Lessons, Streaks)
- Clear XP/reward systems
- Achievement badges
- Premium XP boost (monetization hook)

**Design Guidelines:**
- Always show "next goal" (3 wins to next rank, 50 XP to level up)
- Celebrate milestones with visual flair
- Provide multiple goals simultaneously (never one-dimensional)

---

### For Socializers (30% - James):
**Priority Features:**
- Friend system with easy invites
- Private lobbies (room codes)
- Sharing tools (achievements, streaks)
- Safe communication (preset emotes)
- Clubs/guilds (post-launch V1.2)

**Design Guidelines:**
- Reduce friction for playing with friends (one-tap invite)
- Support asynchronous play (send challenge, they play later)
- Enable shared experiences (team tournaments, club leaderboards)

---

### For Explorers (20% - Aisha):
**Priority Features:**
- Tutorial lesson library
- Cultural storytelling content
- Daily puzzles
- AI analysis (post-launch)
- Replay system (post-launch)

**Design Guidelines:**
- Provide depth, not just breadth
- Explain "why" behind strategies
- Authentic cultural representation
- Gradual complexity curve

---

### For Killers (10% - Marcus):
**Priority Features:**
- ELO rating system
- Competitive matchmaking
- Global leaderboards
- Tournaments (post-launch)
- Spectator mode (post-launch)

**Design Guidelines:**
- Fair competition (no pay-to-win)
- Visible skill measurement
- High-stakes events
- Recognition for top performers

---

### For Families (Kai + Parents):
**Priority Features:**
- Safe environment (no free text chat)
- Private lobbies
- Clear tutorials
- Short match times (5-10 min)
- Beautiful, inviting design

**Design Guidelines:**
- Age-appropriate content (E for Everyone)
- Educational value (parents approve)
- Family-friendly monetization (no exploitative tactics)
- Parental controls (future consideration)
