## ‚ö†Ô∏è Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Oware unknown in Western markets
**Mitigation:** Position as "ancient strategy game" like chess, emphasize learning curve is gentle, cultural storytelling creates intrigue

### Risk: Multiplayer complexity delays launch
**Mitigation:** Use Photon (proven middleware), focus on turn-based (simpler than real-time), defer tournaments to post-launch

### Risk: Premium conversion rate too low
**Mitigation:** Generous free tier drives volume, 7-day free trial reduces friction, paywall at high-value moments

### Risk: Moderation challenges (ages 7+)
**Mitigation:** No free text chat at launch (preset emotes only), private lobbies for families, clubs post-launch with moderation tools

### Risk: Asset quality doesn't match "stunningly beautiful" vision
**Mitigation:** Prototype-first validates fun, then invest in art, Asset Store for polish elements, hire freelancers for key assets
